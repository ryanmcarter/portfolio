About

I currently am a Senior Product Designer at New York Shipping Exchange (NYSHEX) working exclusively on design systems since January of 2023.

Prior to NYSHEX, I was one of two Senior Systems Designs at Ribbon Home responsible for designing and delivering a custom design system.

Before that, I was the only designer at Shoflo, designing live event production software and their design system.

When not tinkering with Figma, I am working on a fintech app (thyme), a community accessibility resource (a11y initiative), or on my 10+ year freelance client project helping usher in the next era of entrepreneurs.

I have been designing for 10 years with a focus on creating accessible design systems. Outside of tech, I enjoy baking, fermentation, coffee, gardening, EDM, and travel.
